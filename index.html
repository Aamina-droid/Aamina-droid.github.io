Become a crochet connoisseur

Read time: 5 minutes

Have you ever thought you needed a new bag for uni? A hat for the chilly London weather? Or even a some cute keychains for a birthday present? This page will give you all the insider tips and tricks to tangle yourself up in.

EQUIPMENT: Crochet hook (size depends on yarn and pattern), yarn, sewing needle, scissors, optional stitch markers

To begin, click the 'Straight from the start' link at the navigation bar found in the header. This will tell you how to start any crochet project. This requires minimal practise; it is very straightforward.

Next, click the stitches link in the top navigation bar. This will direct you to my page on different stitches you may utilise in crochhet projects. Note that this is not exhaustive, there are tons of other stitches and patterns you can create with your yarn and hook, but I will teach you the basics used in almost every design.

Once you have done this, you can click my pattern page to start your first project! Mine was a laptop case, feel free to copy this pattern by following my link!
